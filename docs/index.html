Huricane Recovery

In Florida, the home state in which Zinklof.Dev was founded and still resides. hurricane Helene and Milton came crashing through, destroying infastructure, taking lives, flooding, taking everything from some people in a few quick moments. Not only was florida affected, Helene went north, hitting states like Alabama, Georgia, and South + North Carolina especially hard. Zinklof.Dev is fortunate to not have lost much at all through these hurricanes, but we know others were not as fortunate, so this disaster relief link stays up even now well after the hurricanes.

All donations are through American Red Cross, Zinklof.Dev will not gain from your donation, specifically their disaster relief, which will also go to any other disasters that may take place In America, not just hurricane relief.
